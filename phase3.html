Animation Station

Welcome to Phase 3! Here, you can relax, and just watch your shape move by itself. On the side, you will see a line, probably with lots of differnt points and edges! This line is totally random, and if you want a different one, just press the 'Change Shape' button above, and you'll see it morph into something different! Sometimes, your shape might act a little weird after changing, so please be kind, and don't change its path too often!
